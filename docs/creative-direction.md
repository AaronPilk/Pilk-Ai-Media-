# Creative Direction — Pilk.ai Media

## Concept: The Living Website
Visitors move through the creation of a website: raw possibility → strategy → structure → design → interaction → launch → growth. Abstract digital material (liquid glass, chrome, wireframe, floating browser frames) organizes itself into finished websites as you scroll. Fluid, never chaotic.

## Brand
- **Name:** Pilk.ai Media · **Domain:** pilk.ai
- **Personality:** innovative, confident, precise, creative, premium, direct, human.
- **Positioning:** design-studio quality + practical conversion/marketing execution.
- **Core message:** We build websites people remember and businesses can actually use.
- **First audience:** real-estate agents, luxury agents, teams, brokerages, mortgage, title, local business — architecture stays broad for other industries.

## Palette (white / dark purple / black)
- `--bg`: near-black `#0A0A0B`
- `--surface`: warm off-black `#0E0E12`
- `--surface-alt` (light sections): bone `#F4F2EE`
- `--text`: `#F7F6F4` / muted `#A6A3AD`
- **Accent (dark purple):** `--accent #7C3AED` core, deep `#4C1D95`, glow tint for light only. One accent, used sparingly.
- Materials: chrome, liquid glass, translucent resin, satin metal, wireframe, soft light. Subtle grain, thin precise borders, modest radii, wide soft shadows.

## Typography (max two families)
- **Display:** Space Grotesk (geometric, confident).
- **Body:** Inter (neutral, legible).
- Fluid `clamp()` scale (see `globals.css`). Type carries the identity.

## Do / Don't
**Do:** editorial asymmetry, quiet moments between effects, project previews as the visual focus, light/dark environment shifts, purposeful motion.
**Don't:** neon blue/purple spam, radial-gradient soup, excessive blur, hundreds of glowing dots, HUD clutter, fake code, tiny illegible text, animation as a substitute for hierarchy, desktop-only experience.

## Voice
Clear, direct, premium, short, specific. Banned phrases: "elevate your digital presence," "unlock your potential," "cutting-edge," "in today's digital landscape," "seamless synergy," "game-changing," "revolutionize," "where creativity meets technology." Preferred lines live in `src/content/site.ts`.

## Labels & honesty
Conceptual designs are labeled Template / Concept / Available Design / Starting Layout — never "Client Project" until real work exists. No invented awards, stats, reviews, or revenue.
