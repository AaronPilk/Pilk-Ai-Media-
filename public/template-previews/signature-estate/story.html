Our Philosophy

A house is never just a transaction.

We built Maison Laurent on a single conviction — that the finest residences deserve to be represented as the works of art they are.

Where most firms list, we curate. Every property we take on is photographed, filmed, and presented with the same intention an architect brought to its design. We work with a small number of clients each year, by deliberate choice, so that nothing is rushed and nothing is ordinary.

Discretion is the foundation of everything we do. Many of the homes we represent never reach the open market — they move quietly, between people who understand their value. That trust is the firm's truest asset.

The Principal

Élise Laurent.

Élise founded the firm in 2003 after a decade in fine-art advisory, bringing a curator's eye to a market that had grown impatient and impersonal. Her approach is quiet, exacting, and entirely client-led.

She represents a small, deliberately limited roster of sellers and buyers across Tampa Bay and the Gulf coast, and is regularly entrusted with off-market estates that never appear publicly. Her reputation is built on what she does not say.
