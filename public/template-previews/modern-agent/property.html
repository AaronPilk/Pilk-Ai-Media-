About this home

Set directly on Tampa's iconic Bayshore Boulevard, this 4-bedroom waterfront residence pairs classic curb appeal with a fully reimagined interior. Floor-to-ceiling windows pull the water into nearly every room, and an open layout makes it built for both quiet mornings and easy entertaining.

The chef's kitchen anchors the main floor with quartz counters, a full pro appliance package, and a walk-in pantry. Upstairs, the primary suite opens to a private balcony with unobstructed bay views. Outside, a resort-style pool and covered lanai sit just steps from the seawall.

We launched with professional twilight photography, a full video walkthrough, and a targeted paid campaign aimed at qualified South Tampa buyers. The result: 14 showings in the first weekend and a full-price offer accepted on day nine.
