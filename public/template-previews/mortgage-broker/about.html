Our story

Built by people who got tired of the runaround.

Beacon Lending was founded in 2014 by a small group of loan officers who'd spent years inside big banks watching good borrowers get treated like file numbers. Applications would stall for weeks. Calls went unreturned. Fees appeared out of nowhere on closing day.

We decided to build the lender we wished our own families had used — one that quotes honest rates, explains every line, and answers the phone. No call centers. No bait-and-switch pricing. Just a loan officer who owns your file from application to keys.

A decade later, we've helped more than 11,000 families buy and refinance across the country, and the standard hasn't changed: tell people the truth, move fast, and close on time.
