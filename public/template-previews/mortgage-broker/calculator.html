How it works

What goes into your payment

A monthly mortgage payment is more than principal and interest. Here's what we factor in.

P&I

Principal & interest

The core of your payment — what you borrowed plus the cost of borrowing it, spread evenly across your loan term.

T+I

Taxes & insurance

Property taxes and homeowners insurance are usually collected monthly and held in an escrow account, then paid on your behalf.

PMI

Mortgage insurance

If you put down less than 20% on a conventional loan, PMI applies until you reach 20% equity. We estimate it at roughly 0.5% of the loan per year.
