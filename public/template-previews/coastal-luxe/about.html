Our story

Born on the islands, by the water our whole lives.

Shoreline & Co. started the way most good things on the coast do — slowly, and on a dock. We grew up fishing these flats, learning which canals stay deep at low tide and which beaches go quiet after Labor Day. When we started selling real estate, we refused to do it like everyone else: no pressure, no script, no agents who've never set foot on the property.

Today we're a small team on purpose. We'd rather take fewer clients and actually know your name, your boat, and the exact corner of the coast you've been dreaming about. That's how you end up on the right street — not just the right MLS listing.
