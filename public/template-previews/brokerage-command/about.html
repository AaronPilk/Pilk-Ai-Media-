Our story

A brokerage built around the client.

Meridian Group started in a one-room St. Pete office in 2014 with a simple idea: agents do their best work when the brokerage actually has their back.

Where we started.

Jordan Reyes left a national franchise frustrated by the same thing every agent complains about — paying for a big name while getting nothing back. No marketing, no leads, no support. Just a logo and a desk fee.

So in 2014, Meridian opened with four agents and one rule: invest in the people closing the deals. Build the marketing, the tech, and the support staff in-house, then hand it to agents for free.

A decade later, that bet has turned into 48 agents across three offices, $1.2B in closed volume, and one of the highest agent-retention rates in Tampa Bay. The rule hasn't changed.

What we stand on

Three things every agent and every client can count on.

Service over volume

We'd rather close 1,900 deals well than 5,000 sloppily. Every client gets an agent who answers the phone and tells the truth.

Invest in the agent

Marketing, leads, tech, and a coordinator — provided, not billed back. When agents win, clients win, and the firm grows.

Know the market cold

We only work Tampa Bay. Deep local knowledge beats a national footprint every time you're pricing a home or writing an offer.
