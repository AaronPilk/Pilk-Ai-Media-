A bright, move-in-ready three-bedroom on a quiet cul-de-sac in the heart of Riverside. The open-concept main floor connects a renovated kitchen with stainless appliances to a sun-filled living room and a private back patio — ideal for entertaining or a low-maintenance morning coffee.
