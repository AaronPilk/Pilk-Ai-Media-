FAQ

Tenant questions, answered

What do I need to apply? +

A government-issued ID, proof of income (recent pay stubs, an offer letter, or bank statements showing income of roughly 3x the monthly rent), and contact info for prior landlords. Each adult 18+ submits their own application and pays the $45 screening fee.

How long does approval take? +

Most applicants receive a decision within 48 hours once all required documents are submitted. We'll keep you updated by email and text throughout the process.

What's the security deposit? +

Standard deposits are equal to one month's rent. Well-qualified applicants may be offered a reduced deposit. The exact amount is listed on each rental's detail page.

Can I have pets? +

Many of our homes are pet-friendly. Pet policies, deposits, and monthly pet rent vary by property and are listed on each listing. Service and assistance animals are always accommodated per fair-housing law.

How do I pay rent? +

Through your tenant portal. Free ACH bank transfer or pay by card. You can enable autopay, split rent with roommates, and view your full payment history anytime.

What if something breaks after hours? +

Submit a request through the portal anytime, or call our 24/7 emergency maintenance line for urgent issues like leaks, no heat, or lockouts. We dispatch licensed, insured vendors fast.
