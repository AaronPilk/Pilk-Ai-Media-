The Residence

Water on three sides, light from every direction.

Set on a rare point lot on Davis Islands, The Bayfront Residence is a study in restraint and scale. Floor-to-ceiling glass dissolves the line between interior and bay, while warm stone, white oak, and bronze detailing ground the home in something timeless. Designed for slow mornings on the water and effortless evenings under Tampa's amber light, every room is oriented toward the view it deserves.

Beyond the architecture is a way of living: a private deepwater dock at your door, a chef's kitchen built for gathering, and a primary suite that feels less like a bedroom and more like a retreat. This is a home for those who have stopped looking for the next thing.
